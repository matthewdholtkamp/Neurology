# Neuro Scutbook

A bedside neurology reference for **MDs, NPs, PAs, and medics** — how to work up and
manage a patient with neurological symptoms in the clinic, ER, or on the wards, the way
a neurology consult would. Each topic pairs a practical **consult workflow** with a
plain-language **evidence brief**, plus a standardized **military-medicine layer**
(deployability, profiles, EPTS/LOD, retention, MEB/IDES).

!!! danger "Disclaimer — read first"
    This site is **educational clinical decision-support**. It is **not** a substitute for
    professional clinical judgment, formal consultation, or individual medical advice, and
    it does **not** establish a provider–patient relationship. Dosing, protocols, and
    military standards change — **verify everything against current guidelines, product
    labeling, and the governing regulation before acting on it.** Use reflects your own
    professional responsibility.

## How to use this

- **On call / at the bedside?** Open the topic and read top-to-bottom: red flags →
  rapid approach → workup → management → order set → disposition.
- **Studying or teaching?** Jump to the **Discover** section at the bottom of each topic
  for a current evidence summary and the key trials.
- **Military provider?** Every clinical page carries a **Military medicine** box for the
  deployability and disability-evaluation implications.
- **Search** (top of page) is the fastest way in — type a symptom or drug.
